At first I didnt know what i was doing so i just started researching, Luckily for me I found a youtube channel that talks directly about it, although i felt like i was cheating, it was better for me to take a first step than keep on waiting
So i did the challenge under that youtube channels guidance and it was pretty fun, i started to learn more about SSH, PicoCTF's webshell and the many tiny details

what i learned:
CTRL SHIFT D is for pasting in a webshell, same for CTRL SHIFT C for copying.
In the webshell i found that passwords are actually invisible, so it stumped me when it didnt show up at first, so i just kept redoing it until i figured out the problem was, apparently in PicoCTF webshell, passwords are kept invisible, so all i had to do was press Enter, which made me glad

I finished the CTF challenge in 11 minutes, although slow, i learned alot. This was a great first step for me. Ill try to do the challenges without guidance after this, as now i have a foundation and some knowledge on how it works, i cant say for sure for other challenges but ill try my best
